# MeditateOS 1.0
**MeditateOS 1.0** is the first major operating system of meditateOS. First released to the public on Scratch on 14 February 2025, it has been succeeded by MeditateOS 2.0.
## Editions
MeditateOS has 5 editions, 2 of it being remixes of Azhel-Sharp, a constructed programming language in Scratch developed by DaRender.
### MeditateOS 1.0 No Media
Developed for the European market (there isn't any European market for Scratch operating systems) as there isn't a built-in media player.
### MeditateOS 1.0 Root
Designed for those who despise bloatware. Just the Trash app and very simple background and 'calmbar' (meditateOS equivalent of the Menu Bar in macOS)
### MeditateOS 1.0 AZ+
Includes the AZ+ (Azhel Sharp) language developed by DaRender. Fix 1 was however added by me.
